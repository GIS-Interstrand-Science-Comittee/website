About Our Program

The Laptop Refurbishing Program takes donated computers, tablets, and other digital devices, refurbishes them to optimal working condition, and distributes them to individuals and communities in need. We believe that access to technology is a fundamental right in today's digital world.

Our skilled volunteers clean, repair, upgrade, and test each device before installing new software and preparing it for its new owner. Through this process, we not only help bridge the digital divide but also reduce electronic waste by extending the life of technology.

Donate

Donate your used laptops, tablets, smartphones, or other devices. We accept technology in any condition, working or not. Every donation helps us provide more devices to those in need.

Refurbish

Our team of tech volunteers repairs hardware issues, cleans the devices thoroughly, and installs fresh software to ensure everything runs smoothly for the new user.

Distribute

We work with schools, community centers, and social service organizations to identify recipients who would benefit most from access to technology.

Alternative E-Waste Disposal Options

Can't donate to our program? There are still responsible ways to dispose of your electronic waste to minimize environmental impact and ensure proper handling of potentially hazardous materials.

Senheng e-Waste Recycling Program

You can dispose of your e-waste at your nearest Seheng or SenQ outlet, with the chance to get cash vouchers for non-bulky items. For bigger items, you can contact their free collection service to donate those as well.

ERTH: Electronic Recycling Through Heroes

ERTH is one of the more legitimate recycling service you could reach out to. They are recognized by the Department of Environment (Jabatan Alam Sekitar) as a Top Authorised Collection Centre, and works with a government-licensed recycling facility to dispose your e-waste.

They take in all varieties of e-waste, including laptops, desktops, phones, tablets, home appliances, and flatscreen TVs. The best part about all this is that you get paid! ERTH will pay you for your contributions via bank or e-wallet upon collection of your e-waste. You will even get free pick up service if you recycle a minimum of 3 non-bulky devices.

UrbanR Recycle+

UrbanR Recycle+ is a recycling collection service which will go through the hassle of segregating your unwanted waste. Once collected, they will decide whether to refurbish the items, salvage the spare parts, or send them for recycling.

IPC Recycling and Buy Back Centre

The IPC Recycling and Buy Back Centre in IPC Shopping Centre is a favourite for those who frequently recycle. They are well-organized and their recycling labels are clear and easy enough for anyone to understand.

Karun Hijau Recycling

Karun Hijau is a recycling organisation who treat recycling as a last resort; they always try to find ways to reuse or repurpose the item (e.g charity, turning them into art, sell as secondhand)
